Willa Kate Productions is a Missouri-based collaborative film foundation supporting emerging and underrepresented filmmakers through shared resources, mentorship, and regional partnerships.

With a central hub in St. Louis and projects across rural Missouri, we connect filmmakers working in different places and contexts—building pathways for independent film beyond traditional production centers.

What We Do

We operate as a flexible production platform, bringing together directors, writers, and crew on a project-by-project basis.

Each film is led by its own creative voice, supported by a consistent network of collaborators.

  • Shared production support
  • Mentorship and creative collaboration
  • Projects spanning urban and rural communities

Our Network

Our work is grounded in an ongoing collaboration between filmmakers, producers, and educators across Missouri, including Pearce Healey and Trevor Juenger, alongside a growing circle of creative partners.

Our Focus

We prioritize access—supporting emerging filmmakers and expanding opportunities across geographic and cultural boundaries.

This includes work with St. Louis–based filmmaker Sarah Blair and other artists developing projects both within the city and in rural communities.

Building a statewide filmmaking ecosystem through collaboration, mentorship, and shared infrastructure.

Building a Regional Ecosystem

Willa Kate Productions is building a connected, statewide filmmaking network—linking St. Louis with rural Missouri and strengthening independent film through collaboration, mentorship, and shared infrastructure.
